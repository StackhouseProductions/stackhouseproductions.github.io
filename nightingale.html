About the Project
Nightingale is a contained horror short driven by sound and escalation. What begins as a quiet late-night study session quickly unravels as something unseen forces its way in, pushing two friends into a situation they can’t fully understand or control.
Director’s Note
Nightingale hits hard. A quiet night becomes something it shouldn’t. It begins with curiosity, but some things are better left unknown.
