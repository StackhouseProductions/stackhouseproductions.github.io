About the Project
Sorry, Friend is a psychological horror short centered on isolation, exhaustion, and loss of control. Set over the course of a single night shift, a routine workday begins to collapse as something unseen makes its presence unavoidable. The film explores paranoia, burnout, and what happens when reality begins to slip.
Director’s Note
Overworking yourself is the real killer. It doesn’t hit all at once, but it always catches up.
