STACKHOUSE PRODUCTIONS

ABOUT

Stackhouse Productions is focused on original narrative storytelling, with an emphasis on tension, atmosphere, and unsettling ideas that linger. The company is currently developing a slate of genre-driven projects, with upcoming work centered on horror, suspense, and darkly human situations.

COMING SOON

STILL
After a violent home invasion backfires, two masked intruders are forced to work alongside their own hostages—a pair of art students—when the sculptures inside the house come to life and begin hunting everyone in sight.
SORRY, FRIEND
An overworked insurance auditor believes she is spending another long night in the office alone, until a voice starts speaking to her from behind the bathroom door—and refuses to let her leave.
NIGHTINGALE
During a late-night study session, two college students brush off the sound of a songbird echoing from somewhere inside their apartment, until the presence calling to them steps out of the darkness and attacks.
