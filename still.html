About the Project
Still is a contained horror short where movement becomes the threat. Set inside a small apartment, a failed home invasion spirals into something unnatural as the art students’ sculptures begin to move on their own. The film is built around tension, control, and the fear of what happens when you can’t afford to move.
Director’s Note
Still is built on the terror of seeing something you weren’t meant to see. There are no jump scares, only the sense that you were never meant to be there.
